Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Hairstyle Changer work?

You upload your photo, and our AI simulates different hairstyles on your image. You can preview the results instantly.

Is my uploaded image stored?

No, your image is processed for the hairstyle simulation and is not stored permanently.

Is the service free?

Yes, you can try on hairstyles for free. Some advanced features may be available in a premium version in the future.

What image formats are supported?

We support common image formats such as JPG, PNG, and JPEG.

Can I use the app on my phone?

Yes, our website is mobile-friendly and you can also download our app from the App Store or Google Play.

Who can I contact for support?

You can contact us here or email support@hairstylist.com.
